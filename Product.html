Introducing Memoria™

People suffering from dementia want to remain independent an in control, but are hindered by their illness. In this digital age being independent means being able to use computers efficiently. One of dementias biggest challenges are its effects on short-term memory. Having a diminished short-term memory can be devastating. People diagnosed with dementia need a tool to help them remember.
