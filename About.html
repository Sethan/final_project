The People Behind Memoria™

WE EXIST TO MEMORIZE

Ilham Jilani Abdul-Wakhid Idris Hamed Abdi-Fatah Guled Abdullahi Lars Bjertnes Siham Sidali

You may wonder who Memoria is, well we are 7 students from Hioa all with different backgrounds but with the same wish, and the wish is to make lifes of people with dementia easier. Since we can’t help them regaining their memory, can we help them saving and storing valuable factors in their daily usage of web. Since our society is evolving to a more digital world and almost everything is happening in the digital world, like paying bills or communicating with other people, do we wish to make people with dementia independent and in control of their life. How did we come up with our project ide you may ask? After we were told to find a problem and a solution to the same problem for our final project, one of our co-student told us a story about his meeting with his granddad. He told us how his grandpa had problems remembering him, like if he never knew about him. Afterwards he could use the web properly and had to ask for assistant from his grandmother. The problem for him was remembering web pages he used a day, an hour before. With this story, we came up with Memoria.
