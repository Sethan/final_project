The People Behind Memoria™

WE EXIST TO MEMORIZE

Creation

The motives behind Memoria is to help elderly with a memory problems. We came across this idea when a colleague told us about his grandfather. An elderly man who suffers from short time memory loses, who struggles to remember names of family members, normal daily tasks like paying bills online etc.

Oct 30

Development

Our focus became about equality, and accessibility as we learned increasingly about a disease called Dementia which is the main cause of memory loss to elderly people. We also learned more about the struggles that people with dementia face every day.

Nov 10

Publication

As we strove towards our goal in creating dementia-friendly internet solution. We had gathered as many information as possible regarding the task at hand. We sometimes had faced challenges and difficulties that gave us even a more reason to strive for, and in the end Dementia helper was born.

Nov 30
