THE DEMENTIA HELPER

Your guide to easier and better browsing

Dementia is a umbrella term for a set of symptoms that is linked with brain disorders and memory loss. When people talk about dementia, they often mean dementia caused by Alzheimer’s disease.
People suffering from dementia caused by Alzheimer’s has a reduced short-term memory. This includes forgetting appointments, keeping track of important items such as wallets and mobile devices.
Dementia is unfortunately an incurable disease, and the patients conditions gradually worsen over time.
